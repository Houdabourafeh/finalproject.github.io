Learning Objectives:

            Using the content on the website, MIST students will be able to:

  1. Create an Edpuzzle account with 100% accuracy.
  2. Add content to the classroom with 90% accuracy.
  3. Customize a YouTube video in Edpuzzle with 95% accuracy.
  4. Assign a pre-made or customized Edpuzzle video as a lesson to classroom with 95% accuracy.


Edpuzzle is an educational platform that enables teachers to create and share annotated video lessons with their students.

Teachers can use YouTube videos or upload their own, add questions, notes, and voiceovers and share with their students.

Students watch the annotated videos and provide textual and audio responses.

Teachers can assess student responses, save their students' scores, and organize upcoming video sessions using Edpuzzle Gradebook.
